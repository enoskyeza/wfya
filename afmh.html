All For Mental Health

Mind Your Mind

All for Mental health is a multidisciplinary health professional student initiative that aims to counter the burden of mental health issues especially among youth and adolescents.

The project is implemented under Way forward Youth Africa. The project has a community of youth advocating for advancement in mental health of counter parts and community at large. Through extensively coordinated campaigns and discussions dubbed #mindyourmind, the project has taken a concerted effort to raise awareness of mental health issues in Uganda and Africa as a whole.

Our goal as All for mental health is to reduce the burden of mental illness among youth that often manifests in the sky rocketing numbers of depression, anxiety, substance abuse as well as suicidality amongst people of this age group. Our journey on this quest started in July 2020 when we held our first activity that was an online mental health awareness that ensured that we reached out to 3 million people through the different social media platforms and our webinars. Our success as a young generation have largely come through partnerships with established entities that have supported us materially and technically. In this ever-changing world, innovation and technology should be at the heart of solving the problems we face in our day-to-day life so we always looking better and more efficient ways for how best we can solve the mental health debacle
