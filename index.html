About WFYA

Way forward youth Africa is a youth-led not-for-profit organization that aims to bring about a transformational change in the African health sector through innovation and advocacy.

Together with our partners, and team we work on numerous projects to translate that commitment into practical action, focusing special effort on reaching out to the Youth.

All For Mental Health

Mind Your Mind

We seek to become a global organization championing mental health through promotion of mental health awareness, providing a safe space for the victims and ensuring access to mental health services.

All for Mental health is a multidisciplinary health professional student initiative that aims to counter the burden of mental health issues especially among youth and adolescents. The project is implemented under Way forward Youth Africa. The project has a community of youth advocating for advancement in mental health of counter parts and community at large.
