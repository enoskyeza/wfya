Welcome to Way Forward Youth Africa

Driving transformational change in the health sector of Africa

Way forward youth Africa is a youth-led not-for-profit organization that aims to bring about a transformational change in the African health sector through innovation and advocacy.

The organization was founded in 2021 following the successes of the ALL FOR MENTAL HEALTH initiative that had just successfully held an online campaign to promote mental health awareness in 2020. The success of the campaign was an encouragement to the founders of the initiative who believed they could contribute more to society beyond the mental health space and hence the genesis of the organization which now also houses the initiative.

The organization was registered as a company limited by guarantee on February 3rd 2022 despite having been founded earlier.
